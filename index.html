History

Durham House of Pets is a locally family owned business for the highest quality, fairly traded pet supplies, integrated nutrition and wellness product expertise. Back in 1995, Madison Jonson opened a pet store and offering puppies and kittens for sale to local customers. Twenty years later, we are still family owned and deeply rooted in the central community of New Hampshire. Nowadays, to meet the needs of state's growing community of diversified pet owners. We also offer advanced animal nutrition counseling and a wide selection of supplies for dog, cat, snake, and fish.

Why Choose Us

Because we buy in lots like the big box stores, we are able to give you the satisfaction of supporting a local business while still receiving the most competitive prices and widest selection in durham. Our emplyees possess or are working on degrees in animal science, and most specialize in areas that matter most to your animal's growth and development: nutrition, training, etc. In addition, we go through pet education every month to keep us up to date on what's new so we can answer your questions about your pet's health and behavior. And being a contributing member of our community is just as important to us. We host training programs. We support local rescue organizations and sponsor an annual pet fair to highlight the work of these groups to give humane care to animals in need. So stop by today and select us to be your partner!
