Pricing Match Policy

We're committed to providing the highest quality products at the best prices. If you find a lower price for an identical item at any competitor's store, we'll gladly match it. Durham House of Pets will also match regular and sale prices (excluding Repeat Delivery* pricing) found on Durham House of Pets.com. However, we do not match other online sellers and/or websites.
